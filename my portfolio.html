1971

January 26, 1971

Aygün Kazimova was born in the Baku, Azerbaijan, on January 26, 1971.
She is an Azerbaijani singer-songwriter, dancer, producer and actress.

1988

"Baku Autumn - 88"

She developed her voice early in life.
In 1988, she rose to fame for winning of "Baki Payizi 88" (Baku Autumn - 88).

1990
1994

"First Karabakh War"

In 1990-1994 she took part in the first Karabakh War.

1997

"Ömrüm - Günüm"

Aygün's debut album, "Ömrüm - Günüm", was recorded with "Baku Raks Music" in 1997.

2002
2006

2015

"Azerbaijan's Got Talent"

2016

"Ömrüm - Günüm"

In August 2016, Aygün Kazimova won the award for "Best Azerbaijani Female Act" at the Daf Bama Music Awards.

2017

"Crystal Hall in Baku"

In 2017, she was concert at the Crystal Hall in Baku, Azerbaijan.

2018

"Golden Butterfly Awards"

Her studio album "DUY" was released in 2018, featuring hits such as "Duy" with David Vendetta and "S.U.S.
(Remake)" which she performed at the "Golden Butterfly Awards" in Turkey.

2019

With Philipp Kirkorov of “Calma””

Collaborated with Philipp Kirkorov on a remake of “Calma” and released several duets and music videos.

2020

"Crystal Hall and By SS Production"

Issued Crystal Hall and By SS Production albums, as well as a Remakes album.
Duet “İcazəli” with Miri Yusif gained attention. Dedicated the song
“Əsgər marşı” to fallen soldiers during the Tovuz clashes.

2021

"“Bu qadın”, “Yarımdı o”"

Duet “Yarımdı o” with Rəsul Əfəndiyev. Released the single “Bu qadın.”
Ended the year with the Russian-language EP Ya v Nirvane, notable for using
Dolby Atmos sound, and featuring collaborations with Philipp Kirkorov and Emin.

2023

"“Vəfasızlar Unudulmur”,“Dəyməzmiş” and “Çalxala”"

Continued releasing singles and videos, including “Vəfasızlar Unudulmur,”
“Dəyməzmiş,” and “Çalxala.” Maintained an active concert and media presence.

2024

"Dream Fest 2024"

Performed at Dream Fest 2024 and at “Dalğa Beach” concert.
Participated in major brand events such as the “Şeş-Qoşa” campaign concert.
Ranked among the top-earning Azerbaijani artists through live shows and club performances.

2025

"Etiraf"

Celebrated her birthday by releasing the single and music video “Etiraf.”
Continued her presence as one of Azerbaijan’s most prominent pop icons.

Discography
